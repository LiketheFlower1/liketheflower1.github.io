Hey there! I'm Rose. I believe strongly that we make art and write things to keep ourselves well, and art doesn't have to be perfect to be worth making. I make sketchbooks and journals for artists and writers, draw a lot, and when the ADHD takes over, I needlefelt too many toadstools. I also write an ongoing public correspondence with a poet friend, which you can read here, and I dig for illustrations and designs in historical archives, which you can find here. I'm in recovery from an affair with cocktails, a habit of skipping meals, and the belief that art only counts if it's good. I live on a farm in Pennsylvania with a rotating cast of rescue animals who never question my creative output.
