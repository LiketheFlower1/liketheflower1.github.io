Hey there! I'm Rose. I hope you find something here that inspires you to doodle, draw, or work on that book. I make durable, handmade sketchbooks and journals for artists and writers. I draw a lot, write an ongoing public correspondence with my poet friend, and dig for vintage illustrations and designs in historical archives.

I'm in recovery from an affair with cocktails, a habit of skipping meals, and the belief that art only counts if it's good. I live on a farm in Pennsylvania with a rotating cast of rescue animals who have never questioned why the ADHD sometimes takes over and I have to needlefelt a lot of toadstools.

I hope you enjoy your time here.
