Victorian Botanical Engravings

Detailed botanical engravings produced in Britain and Europe during the Victorian era (1840s–1880s), a period when natural history illustration reached extraordinary precision and artistry. These plates were originally published in scientific and horticultural journals to document plant morphology, medicinal properties, and ornamental varieties. All images are out of copyright and freely available in the public domain.
